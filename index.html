About us

Principle Robotics is a small team focused on building useful robots, quickly and safely. We combine academic rigor with pragmatic engineering to deliver reliable systems.

  • Experience across autonomy, perception, and mechatronics
  • Lean prototyping with a clear path to production
  • Transparent communication and shared roadmaps

Core principles

  1. Ship small, iterate fast
  2. Design for reliability from day one
  3. Measure everything; automate where it matters
